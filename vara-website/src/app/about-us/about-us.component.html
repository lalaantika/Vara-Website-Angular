VARA Platforms is a new Media company started in Silicon Valley North by 3 entrepreneurs who saw a need to change the overwrought advertising space. That's why VARA Platforms is dedicated to dissemination of promotional media through electronic formats attached to vehicles in the ride share space, as well as in commercial transportation.

These promotional platforms reward the viewer, as well as the vehicleowner(s) for their participation in this medium. Rewards include direct cash earnings and special discounts to various vendors. VARA Platforms is dedicated to changing how promotional media is broadcast, always keeping in mind that your time is valuable and needs to include compensation for taking time to participate.

Our mission includes hopefully changing the media industry in the future, so that everyone benefits and revenues are not concentrated in the hands of a few but are enjoyed by all who participate.
